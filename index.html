About

Data Services Indonesia (DSI) is a company which provides Real-Time Information, both for Financial Market and Sport Activities.

DSI provides real time information of Forex (Spot & Futures), Indices (Spot & Futures), Commodities, Stock Markets, both for desktop (End User) and data feed (re-distribution purposes).

DSI is a one of Leading Software House in Indonesia which established since year 2010. We have strong competence resources to provide any kind of real-time information and application for Financial Market Activities, and also applications for Sport Activities, including website development, scoring system, with its equipment supply and rental.

Our Commitment

Vision

To become a leading company that serves multi financial products data and information which also serving real time data for sport activities in Asia.

Mission
  • 1. To provide real time data and information.
  • 2. To provide application for financial market & sport activities.
  • 3. To provide sport equipment (for supporting the activities).
Line of Business
  • 1. Providing Real-Time Data (Currencies, Indices, Commodities, and Stocks) in term of Desktop application (for end users) and data feed (for re-distribution purpose).
  • 2. To provide Online Trading Application (Front Office, Mid Office and Back Office).
  • 3. To provide application for Sport Activities, such as Online Competition Registration, Competition Result (real-time), Online Monitoring, and also Online Rating System.
  • 4. Providing sport equipment (live tracking equipment, Paragliding Equipment, Hang Gliding equipment, Paramotor Equipment, etc).
